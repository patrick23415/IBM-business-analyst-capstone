# 📊 IBM Business Analyst Capstone Project

This project is part of the IBM Business Analyst Professional Certificate. It demonstrates a comprehensive business analysis workflow, including requirements gathering, stakeholder analysis, process modeling, data analysis, and risk management.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The objective of this project is to analyze a business case and develop structured solutions using industry-standard business analysis techniques.

This project covers:

- Requirements gathering and documentation
- Stakeholder analysis and engagement planning
- Process modeling and workflow optimization
- Data analysis and dashboard creation
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning
- Business presentation of findings

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Excel (data analysis, RTM, stakeholder analysis)
- PowerPoint (presentation)
- Lucidchart / Visio (process modeling)
- Cognos / Looker (dashboarding)
- Business analysis frameworks

---

## 📌 Project Workflow

### 1. Requirements Gathering & Documentation

- Identified and documented business requirements
- Created a Business Requirements Document (BRD)
- Developed a Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM)
- Prioritized requirements based on business needs

---

### 2. Stakeholder Analysis & Project Management

- Identified key stakeholders
- Developed stakeholder engagement strategies
- Created a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)
- Defined project scope and deliverables

---

### 3. Process Modeling

- Created process flow diagrams
- Developed BPMN and swimlane diagrams
- Identified inefficiencies and improvement opportunities

---

### 4. Data Analysis & Visualization

- Cleaned and analyzed data using Excel
- Created dashboards to communicate insights
- Transformed raw data into actionable information

---

### 5. Risk Assessment & Mitigation

- Identified potential project risks
- Developed a Risk Register
- Conducted SWOT analysis
- Created risk mitigation strategies and contingency plans

---

### 6. Final Presentation

- Compiled findings into a structured presentation
- Communicated insights and recommendations
- Demonstrated end-to-end business analysis workflow
